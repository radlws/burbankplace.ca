Burbank Place Official Handbook

Burbank Place Apartments Limited has rules and regulations set in place to maximize the enjoyment of living here for all shareholders.

Please feel free to download the handbook.

Handbook: Latest Edition (2013)

Email us: burbankplaceboard@gmail.com

The handbook is updated every few years. A General Annual Meeting (AGM) is held once a year. Shareholders can bring forward ideas and cast votes to accept changes to Rules and Regulations.

Potential can buyers view the handbook to see if they make a good fit living in the community.

Please note that the handbook provided here is provided and disclosed by a shareholder, and may be out of date. A newer version may be available but not on this site. Speak to a board member for further clarification.
