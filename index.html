Community Living at Burbank Place Apartments Limited

Burbank Place Apartments is an equity coop located in downtown Hamilton, at 137 Emerald Street South. The community has been providing quality, secure and affordable co-op living in the Hamilton area for over 60 years. Our shareholders all get a chance to make a difference in our community. Members have equal voting rights and make all major decisions. Members are expected to participate running of the co-op.

The apartment building is comprised of 36 units, consisting of bachelor, one, and two bedroom units. Public transportation, schools, libraries, community centres, shopping and a variety of businesses are in close proximity to the co-op. The co-op is registered under the Corporations Act and unit owners hold capital stock of the company that give them exclusive rights to their units. Acquiring shares is a method of real estate ownership similar to that of the newer condominium ownership. The board of directors of the co-op are shareholders appointed to the position at the Annual General Meeting (AGM). They are responsible for overseeing the building's running and maintenance operations.
